What they do

Our partnership

In their words

“Bloomingdale’s extraordinary commitment to the Child Mind Institute has helped us shine a bright light on children’s mental health. Together we’re changing the conversation and making meaningful change for kids and families every day.”

Child Mind Institute Founding
President & Medical Director

Our partnership

In their words

BCRF Chief Scientific Officer

What they do

By providing teachers with a platform to request much-needed materials for their classrooms, DonorsChoose is moving us closer to a nation where students in every community have the tools and experiences they need for a great education.

Our partnership

Since 2021, Bloomingdale’s has helped raise over $150,000 for DonorsChoose, outfitting over 180 classrooms with vital materials such as classroom basics and books.

In their words

“Bloomingdale’s and their customers are a powerful force of generosity, supporting teachers and students across the country by providing resources that encourage learning about mental health, environmental science, and diversity and inclusion. We’re so grateful to partner with Bloomingdale’s for the Shop for Good campaign, ensuring critical learning materials reach local classrooms from coast to coast.”

—Alix Guerrir,
CEO, DonorsChoose

What they do

Prepare FIT students for professional excellence and leadership in design and business. FIT’s strong ethos of humanity, inclusiveness, and values inspires a global perspective, engagement with community, and commitment to sustainability.

Our partnership

We know our students will carry their learnings forward when they emerge as the next generation of creative industry leaders.”
